Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19)

On 11 March 2020, WHO declared Novel Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19) outbreak as a pandemic and reiterated the call for countries to take immediate actions and scale up response to treat, detect and reduce transmission to save people's lives.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can you prevent the spread of the coronavirus disease?

Practice Social Distancing : Maintain a safe distance of at least one Metre between you and other people when in public places.

Does drinking lots of water help flush out COVID-19?

There is no evidence that drinking lots of water flushes out the new coronavirus or the stomach acid kills the virus.

What is the recovery time for the coronavirus disease?

Using available preliminary data, the median time from onset to clinical recovery for mild cases is approximately 2 weeks and is 3-6 weeks for patients with severe or critical disease.

Can I get infected with the coronavirus disease from someone with no symptoms?

The risk of catching COVID-19 from someone with no symptoms at all is very low.
